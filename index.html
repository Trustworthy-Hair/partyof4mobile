What's Partyof4?

Whether you're new in town or just out to meet new friends, Partyof4 can help you fill out that reservation you have to the hottest restaurant with someone who's interesting and friendly. If you're having trouble getting into that new trendy place in your city, we can help you find other like-minded folks who would love for you to join them for the night.

How it works.

Logging in / Signing up

When you first open the app and don't have an account, you can get started right away! Don’t worry about getting anything in the signup form wrong because we’ll let you know through validation on the client and server.

List view & event detail

Take a look at the map or tap the list icon and view a list of nearby events. You can view the details of that event - who’s host, who’s coming, where, and when. If you see something interesting, request to join!

Creating a new event

Don’t see anything you like? By clicking on the plus-sign, search for nearby restaurants with some info from FourSquare. When you find what you’re looking for, you can create an event at that location. Describe what you’re up to, set the planned time, and specify the maximum number of attendees. Other users will then find your event on their apps!

Approving/rejecting people

If you take a look at an event you’re hosting, you can see who wants to join. Take a look at their profile for their interests and ratings, and use that information to approve or decline the request.
