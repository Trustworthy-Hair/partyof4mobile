What's Partyof4?

Find people who are interesting and friendly!

Whether you're new in town or just expanding your social circle, Partyof4 can help you fill out that reservation you have to the hottest restaurant. Or if you're having trouble getting into that new trendy place, join some like-minded folks at their table!

How it works

GETTING STARTED

 

When you first open the app and don't have an account, you can get started right away!

Don’t worry about getting anything in the signup form wrong because we’ll let you know through validation on the client and server.

VIEWING EVENTS

Take a look at the map or tap the list icon and view a list of nearby events.

You can view the details of that event - who’s host, who’s coming, where, and when. If you see something interesting, request to join!

CREATING EVENTS

Don’t see anything you like?

By clicking on the plus-sign, search for nearby restaurants with some info from FourSquare. When you find what you’re looking for, you can create an event at that location. Describe what you’re up to, set the planned time, and specify the maximum number of attendees.

Other users will then find your event on their apps!

MEETING PEOPLE

If you take a look at an event you’re hosting, you can see who wants to join.

Take a look at their profile for their interests and ratings, and use that information to approve or decline the request.
