What's Partyof4?

Whether you're new in town or just out to meet new friends, Partyof4 can help you fill out that reservation you have to the hottest restaurant with someone who's interesting and friendly. If you're having trouble getting into that new trendy place in your city, we can help you find other like-minded folks who would love for you to join them for the night.
